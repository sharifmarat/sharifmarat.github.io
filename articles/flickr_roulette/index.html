Flickr Roulette

2017-07-24
Comments

If you like to browse through random Flickr photos then Flickr Roulette is the thing you are looking for.

The Roulette randomly selects one of the interesting photos (see interestingness) rather than going through all of them.

A list of photos is updated regulary using Flickr API.
